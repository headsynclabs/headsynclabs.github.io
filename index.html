#an introduction
Yes, a place in a device called a computer (laptop) this is where our friendship all started.
because of an 'obligation' that unites us, yep demands from the school (majors).
starting from that department we got to know each other until finally forming a separate circle.
day after day we pass with various conflicts between individuals and groups but that does not make us divided (fortunately).

Until finally that day came, where I wanted to create an organization that deals in the world of technology but at that time I didn’t invite my friends, because yes! embarrassed haha (that’s weird), several months passed and as usual I wanted to talk about unimportant things (and play games of course) on discord with my friends.

There was one message coming in and he was surprised he sent a screenshot of editing the logo for our organization, *OH SHIT (in my heart), there I was very happy to know that someone is interested in joining this organization whose direction is not clear hahaha.

I immediately chat with him and ask him if he wants to join this organization, he was very enthusiastic (but because of custom email) it was not a problem for me.
After chatting at length, we also have a plan to buy a domain for this organization’s website.

Because there are other friends on the Discord channel, I also invite them to join too and the results are very unpredictable, they want! even some of them are willing to create a github account for the sake of joining (because our organization is based on github).
And yes, started doing some small tests and a few projects.

That’s all the new progress in our organization, it may sound ordinary or even disgusting, but it seems to me that it is very impressive considering that previously they were not at all interested in the platform (because it is identical to one individual in our school hahaha).
